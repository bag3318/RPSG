# Update Guide

> This guide will show you how to easily update RPSG.

## Mac (OSX)/Windows

1. Open `Terminal`/`Command Prompt` (depending on OS)
2. Type in the following: `gem update rpsg`
3. Hit <kbd>Enter</kbd>/<kbd>Return</kbd> (depending on OS)
